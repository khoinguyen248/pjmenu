About Us

About Teco VN

Welcome to Teco VN, where the art of hot pot meets exceptional dining experiences. Our journey began with a commitment to providing high-quality ingredients, a unique interactive dining atmosphere, and unparalleled customer service.

A Long Journey

Our Story

Teco’s story concerns culinary passion, innovation, and a commitment to delivering extraordinary dining experiences. Established in the heart of Singapore, our journey began with a simple yet ambitious goal—to redefine the hot pot experience and introduce the richness of Chinese culinary traditions to the city’s vibrant food scene.

Central to our story is a dedication to quality. We meticulously source premium ingredients, ensuring every slice of meat, every fresh vegetable, and every broth reflects the authenticity and richness of Chinese hot pot traditions. The culinary craftsmanship of our chefs, combined with a commitment to continuous improvement, has allowed us to elevate the hot pot experience and captivate the palates of our diverse and discerning patrons.

Vision and Mission

Mission

Our mission at Teco is to create unparalleled dining experiences that transcend the ordinary. We are dedicated to delivering the highest quality, innovation, and hospitality standards in every service aspect.

Vision

Our vision at Teco is to be a beacon of culinary excellence, shaping the future of hot pot dining and setting a new standard for gastronomic experiences.

Values
